# Feature parity checklist: Apex27 vs Savarix

| Section | Feature | Apex27 | Savarix | Notes / Ideas |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Dashboard & KPIs | Dashboard overview cards | KPI tiles for stock, applicants, offers; quick actions and activity feed | Partially available | Add “Today’s viewings” timeline and quick action tiles on Savarix dashboard. |
| Dashboard & KPIs | Activity feed | Recent events with icons and timestamps | Partially available | Improve filtering and surface offers/viewings inline. |
| Properties & images | Property edit with gallery | Single page with details, marketing flags, drag-drop gallery | Partially available | Combine details + marketing tab; add drag-drop ordering and featured toggle. |
| Properties & images | Portal publishing toggles | Per-portal status badges and switches | Missing | Add portal sync indicators and publish controls. |
| Applicants & matching | In-form applicant matching | Sidebar showing match strength with actions | Missing | Build matching panel on property add/edit with badges and quick send/viewing actions. |
| Viewings & calendar | Viewing creation tied to calendar | Form with status, conflict highlighting and shared calendar settings | Partially available | Add conflict detection, status pills, negotiator colour coding. |
| Viewings & calendar | Calendar sharing/working hours | Settings for shared calendars and hours | Missing | Add calendar preferences per user/branch. |
| Offers, deals, and tenancies | Offers list with status badges | Contact-level offer table with actions | Partially available | Surface offers/tenancies on Savarix contact view; add status badges. |
| Contacts/CRM & communication log | Timeline with call/email/notes quick actions | Rich activity feed plus communication logging | Partially available | Add quick Log call/email buttons and iconised events. |
| Marketing / portals / campaigns | Portal feed management | Toggles for Rightmove/Zoopla, completeness checks | Missing | Add marketing readiness checklist and per-portal status. |
| Admin, branding & settings | Branding preview and email template toggles | Logo/colour upload with live preview; user roles table | Partially available | Add preview and clearer role/permission grid in Savarix admin. |
